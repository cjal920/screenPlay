COLIN LARKIN

A writer of stage and screen, I have had the pleasure of seeing several of my plays produced and the privilege of being invited to pitch to a major studio. More recently, I was named a 2018 semifinalist in the Austin Film Festival's screenwriting competition for my period drama feature LEAH ROSE.

In the words of one festival judge, LEAH ROSE "is a masterful work that deserves to be recognized and produced."

Two of my other current works have also placed in well-regarded competitions. My drama pilot FISHTOWN, as a semifinalist in Creative World Awards; and my dramedy feature BETWEEN LINES, as a quarterfinalist in both CWA and BlueCat.
