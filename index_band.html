COLIN LARKIN

writer

A writer of stage and screen, I have had the pleasure of seeing several of my plays produced and the privilege of being invited to pitch to a major studio.

More recently, I was named a 2018 semifinalist in the Austin Film Festival's screenwriting competition for my period drama feature LEAH ROSE. In the words of one festival judge, LEAH ROSE "is a masterful work that deserves to be recognized and produced."

Two of my other current works have also placed in well-regarded competitions. My drama pilot FISHTOWN, as a semifinalist in Creative World Awards; and my dramedy feature BETWEEN LINES, as a quarterfinalist in both CWA and BlueCat.





PROJECTS

LEAH ROSE

Drama | Feature | 100 pages

When the Crash of 1929 shatters the good life she’s always known, a socialite finds purpose through adversity as she fights to make her way in a harrowing new world.

BETWEEN LINES

Comedy/Drama | Feature | 90 pages

It's a race against time for a frustrated writer who attempts to mend his wild ways before an upcoming high school reunion after he learns that a member of his class has just made a splash in the literary world; but is she a rival he seeks to impress or the one who got away?

FISHTOWN

Drama | Pilot | 58 pages

A talented young chef whose abilities have outgrown his gig at a neighborhood dive must choose between loyalty to a mentor and taking his career to the next level with the help of his ambitious wife.
